Study Guide Website Project

Description

As part of my Human-Computer Intereaction course at UCF, I collaborated with a team of four students to plan, design, and develop a study-guide website tailored for UCF's computer science and engineering students. The site provides major-specific resources, including flowcharts, descriptions of challenging courses, curated video playlists to support studying, campus academic resouces, and a collection of free online learning tools.

Skills Learned

  • User-Centered Design
  • Front-End Web Development (HTML)
  • Team Coordination
  • User Research
